LIFE EXPECTANCY

About

This project aims to enhance the understanding of factors affecting life expectancy by developing a comprehensive regression model that incorporates both immunization rates and the Human Development Index (HDI). The project seeks to identify key predictors of life expectancy, providing insights that can help countries prioritize interventions to improve the health and longevity of their populations. By addressing life expectancy through data analysis and evidence-based solutions, this project will contribute to creating sustainable and healthier environments.
